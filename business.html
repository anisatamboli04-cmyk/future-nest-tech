Project Overview

Our client — a local service business — had no online presence and was losing potential customers to competitors who showed up on Google. They needed a professional website that would establish trust, communicate their services clearly, and convert visitors into leads.

We built them a fully custom multi-page website from scratch — featuring a bold hero section, animated service cards, a team section, client testimonials, and a working contact form that sends enquiries directly to their inbox.

The site launched on time, ranked on Google within the first month, and resulted in a significant increase in inbound enquiries within the first 90 days.
