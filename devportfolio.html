Project Overview

Our client was a self-taught developer looking to break into the industry. Despite having solid projects and skills, they were struggling to get callbacks — because their online presence was just a GitHub profile and a LinkedIn with no personal website to point to.

We built them a stunning personal portfolio website that showcased their projects, skills, and personality in a way that made a strong first impression on recruiters. The site featured animated page transitions, a filterable project gallery, a downloadable CV section, and a contact form.

Within 6 weeks of launching the portfolio, they had received 3 interview callbacks and landed their first job offer.
