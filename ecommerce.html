Project Overview

Our client had been selling products manually through WhatsApp and Instagram DMs — a slow, error-prone process that couldn't scale. They needed a proper online store that would let customers browse, add to cart, and pay securely without any manual involvement.

We built a fully custom e-commerce store with a product catalogue, filtering system, shopping cart, secure payment gateway integration, and an order management dashboard. The store was optimised for mobile shopping — where over 70% of their customers came from.

Within the first month of launch, they processed 3x more orders than their previous manual process — with zero errors and a measurably faster checkout experience.
